Emergency Response Guidelines

  • Chemical Accident
  • Fire in a High-Rise Building
  • Oil Spill in the Ocean
  • Explosion in a Manufacturing Plant
  • Structural Collapse of a Building

Chemical Accident

Evacuate people from the affected area immediately

Provide first aid to the affected people

Isolate the affected area and restrict access

Activate the emergency response team and coordinate with local authorities

Implement a decontamination process for people, equipment, and the environment

Provide regular updates to the public and the media

Investigate the cause of the accident and take necessary measures to prevent similar incidents in the future.

Fire in a High-Rise Building

Evacuate people from the affected area immediately using the designated emergency exits

Alert the fire department and coordinate with local authorities

Provide first aid to the affected people

Establish a command center to coordinate the response and communication with the fire department

Provide regular updates to the public and the media

Investigate the cause of the fire and take necessary measures to prevent similar incidents in the future.

Oil Spill in the Ocean

Contain the oil spill using booms and other materials

Deploy skimmers and other equipment to recover the spilled oil

Alert the coast guard and other authorities

Coordinate with local authorities to establish an incident command system

Monitor the spill and its impact on the environment

Implement a clean-up process for the affected area

Investigate the cause of the spill and take necessary measures to prevent similar incidents in the future.

Explosion in a Manufacturing Plant

Evacuate people from the affected area immediately

Alert the fire department and coordinate with local authorities

Provide first aid to the affected people

Isolate the affected area and restrict access

Establish a command center to coordinate the response and communication with the fire department and other authorities
